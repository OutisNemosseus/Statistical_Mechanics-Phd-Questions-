# Problems 1073-1085: Free Energy & Maxwell Relations

Solutions for problems on thermodynamic potentials and Maxwell relations.

## Problem 1073

**Topic:** Helmholtz Free Energy

### Definition

$$
F = U - TS
$$

### Natural Variables

$F = F(T, V)$

### Differential Form

$$
dF = -SdT - PdV
$$

### Key Relations

$$
S = -\left(\frac{\partial F}{\partial T}\right)_V, \quad P = -\left(\frac{\partial F}{\partial V}\right)_T
$$

---

## Problem 1074

**Topic:** Gibbs Free Energy

### Definition

$$
G = H - TS = U + PV - TS = F + PV
$$

### Natural Variables

$G = G(T, P)$

### Differential Form

$$
dG = -SdT + VdP
$$

### Key Relations

$$
S = -\left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial T}\right)_P, \quad V = \left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial P}\right)_T
$$

## Problem 1075

**Topic:** Equilibrium Conditions

### Minimum Principle

| Constraint | Minimum | Equilibrium |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| Fixed $S, V$ | $U$ | Mechanical |
| Fixed $T, V$ | $F$ | Thermal + Mechanical |
| Fixed $T, P$ | $G$ | Full equilibrium |
| Fixed $S, P$ | $H$ | Isentropic |

## Problem 1076

**Topic:** Deriving Maxwell Relations

### Method: Exactness Condition

For exact differential $dz = Mdx + Ndy$:
$$
\left(\frac{\partial M}{\partial y}\right)_x = \left(\frac{\partial N}{\partial x}\right)_y
$$

### From $dF = -SdT - PdV$:

$$
\left(\frac{\partial S}{\partial V}\right)_T = \left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V
$$

## Problem 1077

**Topic:** Applications of Maxwell Relations

### Example: Internal Energy Dependence on Volume

Show that:
$$
\left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial V}\right)_T = T\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V - P
$$

**Proof:**
From $dU = TdS - PdV$ and Maxwell relation.

For ideal gas: $\left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial V}\right)_T = 0$

For van der Waals: $\left(\frac{\partial U}{\partial V}\right)_T = \frac{a}{V^2}$

## Problem 1078

**Topic:** TdS Equations

### First TdS Equation

$$
TdS = C_VdT + T\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V dV
$$

### Second TdS Equation

$$
TdS = C_PdT - T\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial T}\right)_P dP
$$

---

## Problem 1079

**Topic:** $C_P - C_V$ Relation

### General Result

$$
C_P - C_V = -T\frac{\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial T}\right)_V^2}{\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial V}\right)_T} = \frac{TV\alpha^2}{\kappa_T}
$$

where:
- $\alpha = \frac{1}{V}\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial T}\right)_P$ (thermal expansion)
- $\kappa_T = -\frac{1}{V}\left(\frac{\partial V}{\partial P}\right)_T$ (isothermal compressibility)

### For Ideal Gas

$$
C_P - C_V = nR
$$

## Problem 1080

**Topic:** Helmholtz Energy of Ideal Gas

### Derivation

$$
F = U - TS = nC_VT - T\left(nC_V\ln T + nR\ln V + S_0\right)
$$

$$
F = -nRT\ln V + f(T)
$$
