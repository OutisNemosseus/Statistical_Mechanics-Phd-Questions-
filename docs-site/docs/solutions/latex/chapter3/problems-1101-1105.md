# Problems 1101-1105: Atmospheric Thermodynamics

Solutions for problems on atmospheric physics and chemical potential.

## Problem 1101

**Topic:** Adiabatic Lapse Rate

### Derivation

For dry adiabatic process in atmosphere:

From first law and hydrostatic equation:
$$
C_PdT = \frac{dP}{\rho} = -gdz
$$

$$
\boxed{\Gamma = -\frac{dT}{dz} = \frac{g}{C_P} \approx 9.8 \text{ K/km}}
$$

### Values

| Atmosphere | $\Gamma$ |
|------------|----------|
| Dry air | 9.8 K/km |
| Moist air | ~6 K/km |
| Earth average | 6.5 K/km |

## Problem 1102

**Topic:** Atmospheric Stability

### Stability Criteria

Compare environmental lapse rate $\gamma$ with adiabatic $\Gamma$:

| Condition | $\gamma$ vs $\Gamma$ | Stability |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------|
| $\gamma < \Gamma$ | Stable | Parcel returns |
| $\gamma = \Gamma$ | Neutral | Parcel stays |
| $\gamma > \Gamma$ | Unstable | Parcel accelerates |

## Problem 1103

**Topic:** Potential Temperature

### Definition

$$
\theta = T\left(\frac{P_0}{P}\right)^{R/C_P} = T\left(\frac{P_0}{P}\right)^{(\gamma-1)/\gamma}
$$

### Significance

- Conserved in adiabatic motion
- Constant on isentropic surfaces
- $d\theta = 0$ for adiabatic process

## Problem 1104

**Topic:** Barometric Formula

### Isothermal Atmosphere

$$
P(z) = P_0\exp\left(-\frac{Mgz}{RT}\right) = P_0\exp\left(-\frac{z}{H}\right)
$$

Scale height: $H = \frac{RT}{Mg} \approx 8.5$ km for Earth

### Adiabatic Atmosphere

$$
P(z) = P_0\left(1 - \frac{\Gamma z}{T_0}\right)^{g/R\Gamma}
$$

## Problem 1105

**Topic:** Chemical Potential

### Definition

$$
\mu = \left(\frac{\partial G}{\partial N}\right)_{T,P}
$$

### For Ideal Gas

$$
\mu(T,P) = \mu^0(T) + RT\ln\frac{P}{P_0}
$$

### In Mixture

$$
\mu_i = \mu_i^0(T) + RT\ln\frac{P_i}{P_0} = \mu_i^0(T) + RT\ln(x_iP/P_0)
$$

### Equilibrium Condition

For chemical reaction: $\sum_i \nu_i\mu_i = 0$
