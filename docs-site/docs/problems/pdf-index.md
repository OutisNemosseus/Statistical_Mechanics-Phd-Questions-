## Note on Content

The PDF splits are from the book:
> **Problems and Solutions on Thermodynamics and Statistical Mechanics**
> Yung-Kuo Lim (Editor)
> World Scientific Publishing Company, 1990
> ISBN: 978-9810200558

This book compiles PhD qualifying examination problems from major American universities including:
- MIT
- Princeton
- Columbia
- University of Chicago
- UC Berkeley
- Caltech
- And many others

The problems are designed to test graduate-level understanding of thermodynamics and statistical mechanics.
